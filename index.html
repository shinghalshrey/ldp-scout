Alumni Network

Find the right people inside target firms — then reach out before applications open.

How to network effectively using this page:
① Find people — Use the LinkedIn links below to find alumni at each firm. Search by company first, then filter by role. Target people who joined in the last 2–3 years.
② Reach out on LinkedIn — Send a short note (3–4 sentences). Mention your shared school, your background, and one specific thing about their program. Ask for a 20-minute call.
③ Timing — Reach out 6–8 weeks before applications open. Log contacts in My Applications once you've spoken.
