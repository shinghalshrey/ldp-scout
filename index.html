How it works

  1. 01

    Discover the right programs.

    Browse 48 verified MBA-specific LDPs across Europe, the UAE, and beyond. Filter by sector, function, geography, visa support, and stage. The list is curated for MBAs — none of the generic graduate noise.

  2. 02

    See where you actually fit.

    Upload your résumé and let the AI rank every program against your function, sector, geography, and visa profile. Each program gets a tier and a one-line reason citing specific résumé evidence. A six-dimension gap analysis tells you what to strengthen before you apply.

  3. 03

    Find the people who got in.

    Every program has a pre-built LinkedIn alumni search — your school first, the broader network second. Three ready-to-paste outreach templates per program, all under 300 characters.

  4. 04

    Track everything in one pipeline.

    From shortlist to networking to interview to offer. Drag cards between stages, attach contacts and notes, and export every deadline to your calendar in one click.
